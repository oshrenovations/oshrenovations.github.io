Additions

Additions are a huge expense to your biggest investments, whether it's to home or cottage. It needs to be done right before things go horribly wrong

Cottage Additions: Recreational and Seasonal -use cottages are generally simple matters to address. Tight budgets require special considerations if your needs are to be met.

Ground Floor Additions: Adding living space on the ground floor is fairly simple but needs a professional approach or problems will arise.

Upper Floor Additions: Upper floor additions require an intimate understanding of loads various loads that not only the addition will have to withstand, but what the existing structure will experience with a new load added to it. While it's not rocket science, a significant understanding is required to keep it up to building code while meeting your needs.

Structural Changes: Moving walls, opening up rooms or adding living space all require knowledge of building codes that protect you from unforeseen circumstances.

Full Scale: Major additions, from the basement to the roof are major undertakings and should only be done by accomplished contractors with an engineer's approval and oversight.

Permit Approval: Where permits are necessary to proceed with work, drawings need to be submitted for approval. I can help you save a lot of money by providing the necessary drawings needed to gain permit approval.
