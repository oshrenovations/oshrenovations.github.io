About Old School Handyman Renovations

Old School Handyman Renovations is a Saskatchewan owned and operated company that specializes in retrofitting residential homes and recreational cottages in the Regina and surrounding area.

Ideally located in the Village of Bethune, Saskatchewan, 35 miles northwest of Regina, the heartland of our customer base, OSHRenovations was launched with the idea to serve home owners in a manner and with the expertise they should expect from a contractor.

Looking at your home as if it were ours and aiming for the best solution for the best price is the key to our success.

The company accounting policies were designed to be simple and straight forward, so they are easy to understand. Billing is weekly, based on the work completed and the time involved. This preserves the integrity of our work and makes it possible to provide the highest quality of service for your money.

The intention of the company is to add value to your home and also to your experience as a homeowner. With this in mind, Old School Handyman Renovations is one of the very few renovation contractors that encourages your participation at every level that you feel comfortable with; up to and including getting your own hands dirty if you wish.

After all, it is your home...
