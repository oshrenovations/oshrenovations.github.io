To Upload Images to your website (before putting them onto a web page)

Only 1 image at a time can be uploaded to the content manager, so uploading many images can be a little bit labour intensive and time consuming.

Before uploading images, make sure they are formatted for the web (less than 100 KB), and that you know where to find them on your computer.

1) First, with the Content Manager open, select "Image Manager"

2) Select "Upload New"

3) Select "Choose File"

4) Browse your computer (including CD/DVD ROM Drive, Hard Drive(s), etc.).

Select the Image you want to upload.

5) Select "Submit". Keep in mind that the image has to be less than 100 KB or the upload will fail

6) After the upload is complete (it might take a few seconds), select "View Images" to either continue or repeat the process.
